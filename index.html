Rings of Carrigadrohid Ltd trading as Ring Bros are a full service building company servicing the Cork area. We pride ourselves on providing our clients with the best possible level of service & craftsmanship using only the highest quality materials and the best tradespeople. Ring Bros has being in the same family for over 60 years and has a wealth of experience in the construction industry. We are 100% fully insured and we can perform all grant work.

Ring Bros are active members of the Construction Industry Federation (CIF). Ring Bros has a wide collection of successfully completed projects across a variety of sectors including: residential, restoration / refurbishment, hospitality, commercial, industrial and healthcare. We have generated our sterling reputation on our customer focus and hands-on approach. At Ring Bros, we are proud that most of our workload comes from repeat business and that we have a strong reputation for our competitive edge and dependable implementation of all developments undertaken.
